Latitude vs. Temperature (F)

Unsurprisingly, high temperatures are clearly correlated with low (equatorial) latitudes. The large number of datapoints in Northern (positive) latitudes stems from the higher number of cities in the Northern hemisphere due to the larger land area north of the Equator.
